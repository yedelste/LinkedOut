# Privacy Policy for LinkedOut

Effective date: May 27, 2026

LinkedOut is a browser extension that replaces the LinkedIn home feed with a static message.

## Data Collection

LinkedOut does not collect, store, transmit, share, or sell personal data.

Specifically, LinkedOut does not collect:
- Name, email, phone number, or account identifiers
- Browsing history outside extension functionality
- Precise location
- Financial or payment information
- Health information
- Authentication credentials
- User messages, contacts, or files

## How the Extension Works

LinkedOut runs only on LinkedIn pages and modifies page content locally in your browser.
No user data is sent to any server.

## Remote Code

LinkedOut does not load or execute remote code.
All JavaScript and CSS used by the extension are packaged with the extension.

## Third Parties

LinkedOut does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL and the effective date will be updated.
